About

Mr HABUDUBU, known professionally as ASMO ABDULLAH, is an emerging independent rapper and lyricist hailing from Sylhet, Bangladesh, and currently based in Oman. Drawing from his pure Bangali roots and khati Sylheti dialect, he crafts raw hip-hop tracks that blend powerful storytelling with authentic delivery. His music explores themes of resilience, unrequited love, life's struggles, and personal rebirth, often in Bangla and Sylheti, with occasional Hindi releases. As a dedicated artist in the modern South Asian hip-hop scene, ASMO pours his soul into verses that resonate with raw emotion and contemporary sounds. With hits like "JOKER," "ROCKSTAR," and "9 TO 5," he continues to build his legacy through soulful melodies and unfiltered narratives, captivating listeners worldwide.
